Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Easy to make chocolate chip cookie recipe. These cookies are soft, chewy, and absolutely irresistible. This recipe will easily become your go-to!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semi-sweet or milk chocolate
  • 1 cup chopper walnuts

Steps

  1. Step 1: Gather your ingredients, make sure butter is softened and your eggs are room temperature.
  2. Step 2: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Beat butter, white sugar and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth.
  3. Step 3: Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  4. Step 4: Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.
  5. Step 5: Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts if desired.
  6. Step 6: Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Step 7: Bake in the preheated oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes.
  8. Step 8: Cool on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
  9. Step 9: Store in an airtight container or serve!
