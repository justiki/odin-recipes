Grocery Store White Bread

Description

This easy to make grocery store white bread recipe will help you save money on expensive supermarket bread loafs while also tasting great!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup of whole milk
  • 1/2 cup of filtered water
  • 2 1/2 tsp of yeast
  • 3 cups of bread flour + more for dusting
  • 1/2 tsp of fine grain sea salt
  • 1 tbsp of sugar
  • 3 tbsp of unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Step 1: Combine Milk and Water. Heat in microwave on high for approximately 1 minute.
  2. Step 2: Add yeast to liquid mixture, whisk until it has a light foamy consistency. Cover with plastic wrap and let sit for 10 minutes.
  3. Step 3: Combine flour, salt, and sugar, whisk until thoroughly combined. Add yeast mixture and stir/turnover with a spatula for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Add butter to the mixture 1 tbsp at a time and mix for 30 seconds each until you have a smooth dough. Cover with plastic wrap and let sit for 10 minutes.
  5. Step 5: Move dough ball to an unfloured surface and gently shape it into a smooth ball. Place ball back into bowl. Cover with plastic wrap or damp towel and let the dough rise for 2 1/2 to 3 hours.
  6. Step 6: Once dough has risen, punch to release gas. Move dough to a floured surface and roll into a rectangle. Take one side of the rectangle and tightly roll into a cylinder shape. Place dough into a lightly greased bread pan. Cover with damp towel and let rest for 1 hour.
  7. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Place dough in the oven and bake for 35-40 minutes or until golden brown. When done, let rest in the pan for 5 minutes and then transfer to wire rack to cool for 10-20 minutes. Slice and serve with butter, jam, or use for sandwiches.
