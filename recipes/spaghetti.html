Lasagna

Description

An easy an delicious recipe that fits all budgets. Try this one now, and watch your family fall in love. You'll never buy jars of spaghetti sauce again!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • hamburger
  • 1 32 0z can of crushed tomatoes
  • parmesan cheese
  • Italian Seasoning & Basil

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Add italian seasoning to the meat sauce
  4. let simmer for 5-10 minutes
  5. serve
