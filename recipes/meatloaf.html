Lasagna

Description

This isn't like your grandmas meatloaf... This recips is packed full of beef, just the way it should be

Ingredients

  • Hamburger
  • onions
  • ketchup
  • worstershire sauce

Steps

  1. chop the onions
  2. mix the hamburger and onions together. Place into a small bread pan
  3. bake the meatloaf
  4. prepare the sauce
  5. spread sauce over the meatloaf and bake for 5-10 more minutes
  6. serve hot
