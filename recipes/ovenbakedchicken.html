Lasagna

Description

A quick and easy way to bake your favorite chicken! Bake it with some potatoes, root vegetables, or just by itself!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • seasoning
  • olive oil or melted butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees farenheit
  2. Brine the chicken in salt water for 15 minuters, pat dry, place in pan
  3. Brush melted butter or olive oil on both sides of the chicken
  4. Rub seasoning into the chicken
  5. Roast the chicken for 18-20 minutes or until 165 degrees internal temp
  6. Let the chicken rest by covering it with aluminium foil for 5-10 minutes after baking
